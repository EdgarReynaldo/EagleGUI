52 /*! @enum WIDGET_ZORDER_PRIORITY
53  * @brief This enum provides a small range of z depth for widgets, to enable layering
54  *
55  * WIDGET_ZORDER_PRIORITY controls the z order for widgets used by a @ref WidgetHandler.
56  * Widgets with a higher priority appear closer to the viewer and lower priority widgets
